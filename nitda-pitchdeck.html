NITDA InclusiveCodeFest 2023 Hackathon Pitch Deck

Revolutionizing Ride Hailing in Nigeria - A New Era of Efficient and Affordable Transportation

The Problem

The current state of ride hailing in Nigeria is plagued by high costs, inefficiency, and discomfort. Inflation has made popular ride-hailing apps like Bolt and Uber expensive for the average citizen. The 'along' option, while affordable, offers a subpar experience, wasting valuable time and effort.

Our Solution

Introducing Carpool Nigeria, a revolutionary solution to address these challenges. We leverage carpooling to maximize ride efficiency, reduce costs, and ensure passenger comfort.

Market Research

Extensive market research revealed a significant gap in real-time, efficient ride hailing in Nigeria. Existing solutions like DriveInHud and Jekalo target long-distance travel, while Uber's offering falls short of our real-time, in-route carpooling.

Insight

Our vision is to reshape the future of ride hailing in Nigeria, encouraging users to embrace carpooling for a more cost-effective and environmentally friendly way to travel. Safety is a top priority, and we will continuously refine our service to address user concerns.

Thank You

Thank you for your time and consideration. Together, we can revolutionize transportation in Nigeria.
